Did you know? Tacos al Pastor were inspired by Lebanese shawarma, brought to Mexico by Lebanese immigrants in the 1930s.

Tacos al Pastor

2 hours Medium
Street Food Pork Quick Meal

Ingredients

  • Pork shoulder, thinly sliced
  • Chili marinade
  • Pineapple, diced
  • Corn tortillas

Instructions

  1. Marinate pork in chili mixture overnight.
  2. Cook on a vertical spit or pan grill. Serve with pineapple and tortillas.

Did you know? Enchiladas date back to Mayan times when people would wrap food in corn tortillas.

Enchiladas

2 hours Medium
Street Food Pork Quick Meal

Ingredients

  • Corn tortillas
  • Shredded chicken
  • Red enchilada sauce
  • Cheese

Instructions

  1. Fill tortillas with chicken and roll.

Did you know? Chiles Rellenos were first created in Puebla, Mexico, and are considered a national dish.

Chiles Rellenos

2 hours Medium
Street Food Pork Quick Meal

Ingredients

  • 6 poblano peppers
  • 1 lb queso fresco
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup flour

Instructions

  1. Roast and peel poblano peppers, then stuff with cheese.
  2. Dip in egg batter, coat with flour, and fry until golden.

Did you know? Pozole was a sacred dish for the Aztecs, often served during religious ceremonies.

Pozole

2 hours Medium
Street Food Pork Quick Meal

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs pork shoulder
  • 2 cans hominy
  • 6 dried guajillo chiles
  • Garlic and spices

Instructions

  1. Cook pork with garlic and spices until tender.
  2. Add hominy and chile sauce, simmer for 30 minutes.

Did you know? Churros were brought to Mexico by Spanish shepherds and became a popular street food.

Churros

2 hours Medium
Street Food Pork Quick Meal

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • Cinnamon sugar for coating

Instructions

  1. Boil water with sugar, add flour to make dough.
  2. Pipe dough into hot oil, fry until golden, coat with cinnamon sugar.
