Did you know? Dosa is a South Indian staple, often enjoyed with chutney and sambar.

Dosa

30 mins Medium
Breakfast Vegetarian Healthy

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice
  • 1 cup urad dal
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Soak rice and dal, blend into batter, ferment overnight.
  2. Spread batter thinly on hot griddle, cook until crispy.

Did you know? Samosas were introduced to India by Middle Eastern traders in the 13th century.

Samosa

45 mins Medium
Snack Vegetarian Street Food

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 potatoes (boiled and mashed)
  • 1 cup peas

Instructions

  1. Knead flour into dough, make cones.
  2. Fill with spiced potato-pea mix, fry until golden.

Did you know? Paneer Tikka is a popular North Indian appetizer, perfect for parties and gatherings.

Paneer Tikka

40 mins Easy
Appetizer Vegetarian Grilled

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer (cubed)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • Spices as required

Instructions

  1. Marinate paneer in spiced yogurt.
  2. Skewer and grill until golden.

Biryani

2 hours Hard
Main Course Rice Dish Festive

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g meat or vegetables

Instructions
